Discover Aura

Why Aura

Speed, security, scalability

Aura is a fast, high-throughput open-source smart contract platform for digital assets and dApps. It combines pioneering technologies to provide unparalleled security and sustainability to decentralized applications, systems, and societies.

Highly scalable

Aura can process thousands of transactions per seconds and scale to thousands of nodes.

Simply secure

Aura’s validator nodes form a global, trustless, and leaderless Proof-of-Stake network.

Lowest costs

Deploying smart contracts on Aura cost just a tenth of what they cost on Ethereum.

Flexible

Aura enable developers to easily launch blockchains with a wide array of application-specific features.

What’s possible with Aura?

Build Now

Aura is an open, programmable smart contracts platform for decentralized applications.

Build fast, low cost, Solidity-compatible dApps

Launch Ethereum dApps that confirm transactions instantly and process thousands of transactions per second, far beyond any decentralized blockchain platform today.

Launch customized blockchains, private & public

Deploy blockchains that fit your own application needs. Build your own virtual machine and dictate exactly how the blockchain should operate.

Scale to millions of validators with minimal hardware

Stake, or lock up, your AUR to help process transactions and further secure the platform–providing security guarantees well-above the 51% standard. You probably have the hardware required to join the platform.
